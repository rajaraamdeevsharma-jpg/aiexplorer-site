01

Create Your Google Business Profile

Go to business.google.com and create your listing

+
  • 1
    Open business.google.com on your phone or laptop. Sign in with your Google account (create one if needed — use a business email like aiexplorer@gmail.com).
  • 2
    Click "Manage now" → "Add your business".
  • 3
    Business name: Type exactly "AI Explorer — K-12 AI Education, Hisar, Haryana". Example: "AI Explorer — K-12 AI Education, Hisar".
  • 4
    Category: Search for "Tutoring Service" or "Educational Institution". Pick whichever applies.
  • 5
    Select "Yes" when asked if you have a physical location. Even if you teach online, add your home city — this is what makes you show up in local search.
Pro tip: Use your real address for verification (Google will mail a postcard there). You can hide the exact address from public view later — only the city will show.
02

Fill Your Business Info (100% Complete)

Incomplete profiles rank lower — fill everything

+
  • 1
    Phone number: Your WhatsApp number. Parents can call or WhatsApp directly from Google.
  • 2
    Website: Add the link to your enrollment form HTML file (host it free on GitHub Pages or Netlify first).
  • 3
    Business hours: Set realistic hours — e.g. Mon–Sat 9am–7pm. Don't leave blank.
  • 4
    Description (750 characters max): Copy and paste this optimised description ↓

Pro tip: Include your city name naturally in the description — Google reads it for local search ranking.
03

Add Photos — Minimum 10

Listings with photos get 42% more requests

+
  • 1
    Logo photo: Your AI Explorer logo or program name on a clean background.
  • 2
    Cover photo: A student working on a laptop or tablet — bright, clear, happy. This is the first image parents see.
  • 3
    Classroom photos (3–4): Students in class (even if it's on a phone screen showing a Teachable Machine demo). Authentic beats stock.
  • 4
    Project photos (2–3): Screenshots of AI models students built — the chatbot, the classifier, the ethics poster.
  • 5
    Certificate photo: A completion certificate being handed to a student. Parents love this.
  • 6
    Teacher photo: You in front of a whiteboard or with a student. Builds personal trust.
⚠️ Never use stock photos. Google can detect inauthenticity and parents will notice. Real photos — even slightly blurry phone shots — convert 3× better than stock images.

05

Verify Your Listing (Critical Step)

Without verification you won't show up in Maps or Search

+
  • 1
    Google will ask you to verify. Choose postcard by mail (most reliable) or phone/SMS verification if offered.
  • 2
    If postcard: Google mails a card with a 5-digit code to your address. Takes 5–14 days in India. Enter the code when it arrives.
  • 3
    While waiting for verification: keep adding photos, posts, and services. The listing is live but unverified — verification makes it rank.
  • 4
    Once verified: your listing appears in Google Maps and the local search panel when parents search nearby.
Pro tip: After verification, immediately ask your first 5 students' parents to leave a Google Review. Even 3 reviews puts you above competitors with zero reviews.
