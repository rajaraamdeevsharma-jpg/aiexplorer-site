The Session Plan

45 Minutes.
Real AI. Built by kids.

Here's exactly what happens from the moment your child joins to the moment they leave with their project.

1
0–5 min
The Hook — "AI Is Already in Your Pocket"
We open by asking students: "Has anyone talked to Siri or Alexa today?" Then we reveal what's actually happening inside — and students realise AI is everywhere. Curiosity unlocked.
2
5–15 min
The Unplugged Game — "Be the AI"
One student plays the "AI". Others show them objects (a cup, a shoe, a book). The "AI" must learn a secret sorting rule. Students discover pattern recognition without touching a device.
3
15–35 min
Build It — Train Your Own Image Classifier
On any device, students open Google Teachable Machine. They collect 10 images of two objects (apple vs banana, thumbs up vs thumbs down), train a classifier, and test it live on their camera. The room always erupts when their AI works for the first time.
4
35–42 min
Break It — What Makes AI Wrong?
Students deliberately try to fool their classifier — same object, different angle, different lighting. They observe failures and ask: "Why did it get that wrong?" This is AI ethics in real time.
5
42–45 min
Q&A + What's Next
Students share what they built. Parents can ask questions. We briefly share what the full 6-week program looks like — no pressure, no hard sell. Everyone leaves with their classifier saved.
